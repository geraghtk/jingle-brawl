üè¶ Santa Tax

üí∏ Loser's Dividend

üé∞ Endgame (Misfit Lottery)

  • Giftless players enter one by one
  • Draw oldest Misfit (FIFO)
  • Path A: Steal from any player (pay cost)
  • Path B: Auction the Misfit
  • No Loser's Dividend in endgame

üéØ Yield Duels

  • Opener can yield if there's a 2nd bidder
  • Duel becomes Challenger 1 vs Challenger 2
  • Both pay half their bid (rounded down)
  • Opener's name goes back in Draw Bag
