A game of high stakes, low blows, and questionable gifts.

Setup Economy Main Game Special Actions Endgame Costs

üéÑ Setup

  1. Distribute Chips: Give each player 10 chips (or 12 for groups larger than 10)
  2. Draw Bag: Put all player names in the Santa Sack
  3. Head Elf: Choose the organizer as the first Head Elf
  4. Duel Methods: Agree on how duels will be resolved (coin flip, rock-paper-scissors, etc.)

ü™ô The Economy

Jingle Chips

Your currency for bidding and challenging. You need chips to attack but defending is always free!

North Pole Fund (Bank)

Collects all costs and taxes. Also pays out Loser's Dividends.

Santa Tax

If a Duel Pot is 3+ chips:
1 chip ‚Üí Bank (tax)
Rest ‚Üí Winner

Loser's Dividend

When you LOSE a duel: Immediately receive +1 chip from the Bank
Exception: No dividend for misfit, yield, or tie-break duels

Naughty Level

Each gift has a Naughty Level starting at 0. It increases by +1 every time that gift is involved in a duel. Naughty Level determines the Minimum Cost to challenge for that gift.

üéÅ Phase 1: Main Game

Continue while wrapped gifts remain.

1. Unwrap

  • Head Elf draws a name from the bag
  • That player (the Opener) opens one gift
  • Gift starts at Naughty Level 0

2. Sealed Bidding

  • Everyone except the Opener may bid (chips hidden in hand)
  • Reveal simultaneously
If nobody bids: Opener keeps the gift OR can use Grinch's Gambit

If bids exist: Highest bidder becomes Challenger 1. Second-highest becomes Challenger 2. Ties ‚Üí tie-break duel.

3. The Duel

Challenger 1 vs Opener (by default). The Defender chooses the duel method.

Yielding

If there's a Challenger 2, the Opener can Yield (step aside). The duel becomes C1 vs C2.

Pot & Payout

Normal Duel (C1 vs Opener) C1 pays full bid to pot
Yield Duel (C1 vs C2) Both pay half (rounded down)

4. Swap Algorithm

  1. Winner takes the contested gift
  2. If winner had a gift, it goes to the Loser
  3. If loser now has 2 gifts, they choose which one goes to the Misfit Pile
  4. That gift's Naughty Level resets to 0
  5. Whoever's gift went to Misfits becomes the new Head Elf

5. Bag Maintenance

  • Win a gift by challenging? Your name is removed from the bag
  • End up giftless? Your name is added back to the bag

‚ö° Special Actions

Grinch's Gambit

"Why settle when you can steal..."

When nobody bids on your opened gift:
Pay the Minimum Cost to challenge anyone for their gift.
This is a direct challenge ‚Äî no pot is created.

Reindeer Reprisal

"Because reindeer kick back..."

When you LOSE a Grinch's Gambit or Sealed Bid Duel:
You may immediately challenge any gift (except the one you just lost the duel for).
Pay the Minimum Cost to the Bank.
A Reprisal can trigger one more Reprisal (max 2 chain).

üß∏ Phase 2: Misfit Lottery (Endgame)

Starts when all gifts are opened but names remain in the bag.

Giftless players enter one-by-one. The Head Elf draws the oldest Misfit (FIFO) as the target.

Path A: The Steal

  • Challenge any player for their gift
  • Pay the Minimum Cost
  • Win: Take their gift; they get the Misfit
  • Lose: You get the Misfit

Path B: The Auction

Open bidding on the Misfit to everyone.

No Bids?

  • Duel any player you choose
  • Win: Get Misfit for FREE
  • Lose: Get Misfit + pay 1 chip to that player

Bids Exist?

  • Duel the highest bidder
  • Winner gets Misfit + pot (minus tax)
  • If bidder wins and was giftless: Active player goes back in bag
Note: No Loser's Dividend in any Endgame duel!
